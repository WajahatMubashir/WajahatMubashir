## What I Do

I sit at the intersection of web engineering, marketing operations, and revenue systems. My job is to make sure a click on a webpage becomes trustworthy data in a CRM — and that every system in between works cleanly.

At **TCP Software** (B2B SaaS workforce management), I own the technical layer across:

- **Web Engineering** — WordPress architecture, custom plugin/block development, performance, SEO
- **Marketing Operations** — Marketo Engage programs, email templates, lifecycle automation, Marketo Measure attribution
- **Analytics & Tracking** — GA4, GTM (client-side + server-side via Stape), BigQuery, Looker Studio
- **Revenue Systems** — Salesforce sync health, LeanData routing, Bizible attribution, Clay intent data
- **AI Tooling** — Custom MCP servers, Claude Cowork agents, AI-readiness infrastructure
